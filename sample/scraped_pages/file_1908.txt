Keep an eye on what matters Measuring your YouTube Ads campaign is the best way to make sure you’re reaching your goals. And you don’t need to be a data expert to do it. There are a range of real-time metrics you can track for the insights you need. See how many people are viewing your ad, how engaged they are, what actions they’re taking, and more. See if campaigns above a certain budget inspire awareness with our free Brand Lift tool. Am I getting the best possible results? Use marketing mix modeling or work with a Google Measurement Partner to compare your results to other channels. Consideration Are people watching?
